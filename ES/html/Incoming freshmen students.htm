ADMISSION POLICIES

INCOMING FRESHMEN STUDENTS

 

     High school graduates who have passed the NEAt and College Entrance Test and have satisfactory general weighted average shall be admitted to the College. All applicants shall be interviewed by the Guidance Counselor and the Dean of Student Affairs. The following documents are required when seeking admission:

1. Form 138

2. Certificate of Good Moral Character

3. Two pictures (two 1" x 1", two 2" x 2", red background)
