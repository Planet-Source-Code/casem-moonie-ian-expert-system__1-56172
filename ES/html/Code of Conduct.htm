GUIDELINES ON DISCIPLINARY ACTIONS

CODE  OF CONDUCT
 

                   Students are expected to abide by the rules and regulations of the College. Violation of any rule could either result to a major or minor offense. A minor offense results to a suspension from 1 to 3 days. A major offense result to a suspension, a dismissal or an expulsion order. Students are advised to read the Manual for Disciplinary Action for particulars.
