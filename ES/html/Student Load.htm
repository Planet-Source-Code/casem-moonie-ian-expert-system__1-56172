ENROLMENT POLICIES

STUDENT LOAD

 

     The regular load during the trimestral program is 18 units  (excluding ROTC). a student is not allowed to enroll more than two laboratory subjects except their junior and senior year. Academic load of working student shall be limited to 9 - 12 units per trimester. Request for overload shall require the approval of the Registrar.

     A student who incurred a falling grade (5.00) and / or drop to any subject during a trimester will not be permitted to overload during the following trimester to recover his failure.

     A graduating student shall be allowed to carry a maximum of 22 - 23 units during his terminal trimester in the College.

     An overload may also be allowed prior to the last term of the course provided that the load during the trimester will nor exceed the 18 units.
