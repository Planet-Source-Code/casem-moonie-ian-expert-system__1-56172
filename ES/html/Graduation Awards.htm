GRADUATION AWARDS
 

I. STUDNETS LEADERSHIP AWARD

This award is presented to the members of the graduating class whoa have outstanding participation in extra curricular activities which promoted student and colleges welfare. Nominees must have exhibited exemplary character and at least 2 years residence in the College. They should have a minimum weighed average of 2.50 with no failing grade in any academic subject and should have further complied with the rules on minimum load. All nominations must be submitted to the Office of the Students Affairs.

II. OUTSTANDING PROJECT AWARDS

  1. Best Software Design. This award is given to members of the BSCS graduating class who were able to meet the criteria.

  2. Best Design Project. This awards id\s given to members of the BSCoE graduating class who have met the criteria in their design project.

  3. Best Feasibility Study. This award is given to BSBA graduating students who were able to meet the criteria.

  4. Best Thesis. This awards is given to members of the graduating class from the CAS.
