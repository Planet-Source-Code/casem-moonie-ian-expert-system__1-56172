GUIDELINES ON DISCIPLINARY ACTIONS

CLASSIFICATIONS OF OFFENSES AND CORRESPONDING PENALTIES
           

  1. MAJOR OFFENSES. Those that merit suspension, dismissal or expulsion, such as:

    1. Any act which impedes, obstruct and prevents or defeat the right of the student top attend his classes;

    2. Carrying deadly weapon and / or dangerous weapon including improvised weapon, explosive and incendiaries inside the campus;

    3. Exploding of firecrackers pyrotechnics, pillbox bomb, Molotov cocktail and the like;

    4. Drug abuse;

    5. Bringing inside the campus intoxicating drinks, prohibited and regulated drugs;

    6. Use of intoxicating drinks and / or regulated drug inside the campus; coming into the campus and being in the school premises under the influence alcohol.

    7. Possession and / or use of marijuana in any form;

    8. Willful disregard for authority, disrespect, discourtesy and disobedience to any school official, member of the faculty, administration or their respective;

    9. Physical assault and / or physical injuries;

    10. Direct physical assault upon any person;

    11. Organized rumbles and physical assault in band;

    12. Threat and coercion;

    13. Possession, distribution or perusal of pornographic magazines, pictures, films, cartridges, cards, key chains, figurines, and the like;

    14. Robbery and stealing;

    15. Theft and qualified theft;

    16. Extortion;

    17. Malversation of the College and Student organization funds;

    18. Unauthorized solicitations;

    19. Selling of unauthorized tickets, raffles, lotteries, and other fund raising paraphernalia;

    20. Malicious acts committed against others within the campus;

    21. Using profane or indecent language and committing vulgar and offensive acts;

    22. Acts of lasciviousness, voyeurism, exhibitionism and other offensive acts;

    23. Unjust vexation;

    24. Defamation, libel and slander;

    25. Immorality;

    26. Malicious mischief;

    27. Vandalism or destructive of school property belonging to any member of faculty, administration and non-teaching staff;

    28. Forgery and falsification and attempt hereof;

    29. Possession and gambling paraphernalia;
