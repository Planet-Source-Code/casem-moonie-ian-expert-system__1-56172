GENERAL POLICIES FOR STUDENTS

APPLICATION FOR ID CARD, ID VALIDATION

 

     All the students must secure identification cards from the Registrar’s Office. Student must present Identification Cards when entering the school premises properly worn at all times. The student must always be the basis for identifying a students when he transacts business with the College.

     In case of loss of the ID card, a student should execute an affidavit of loss and submit same to the Registrar’s Office. Student pays an amount of P50.00 to the Accounting Office and present the receipt to the Registrar’s Office with his 1” x 1” picture (red background).
