SCHOLARSHIPS, HONORS AND GRADUATION AWARDS

OTHER AWARDS

 

  1. COMMUNITY SERVICE LEADERSHIP AWARD. This awards recognizes civic-minded students with exemplary contribution to the improvement of the quality of life of their fellow Filipinos through their self-less and unremitting service and dedication. Qualification are as follow: cumulative general weighted average (CGWA) of at least 2.5; residence of at least two (2) years at AMA Computer College; active involvement in a community extension project (off-campus) for at least two years; must possess good moral character as certified by the office of the Students Affairs. 

  2. ATHLETICS AWARD. This award honors a students-athlete who had consistently rendered outstanding performance in sport competitions. To qualify students athlete must have a cumulative general weighted average (CGWA) of at least 2.5; residence for at least two (2) years at AMA Computer College; membership in a varsity team at least two (2) years as certified by the Office of the Sports Director and / or Dean of student Affair; good moral character, as certified by the office of the Students Affairs. 

  3. HONORABLE AMABLE M. AGUILUZ, SR. LEADERSHIP AWARD. This award gives recognition to the students with multi-facets accomplishments in various fields of concern. Recipient of this award is envisioned to reflect the wholistic person, which is the core of AMA Computer College’s global mission statement. Nominee to this award must possess all of the following requirements: cumulative general weighted average (CGWA) of at least 2.00 with no failing grade in any subject; residence of at least three (3) years at AMA Computer College; must be an officer or a committee head of one (1) recognized organization on campus for at least two (2) years, as certified by the Office of the Student Campus.

For any given school year, there is only one awardee or recipient to the HONORABLE AMABLE M. AGUILUZ, SR. LEADERSHIP AWARD for all the AMACC campuses nation wide. The campus Chairs of Honors and graduation. Awards Committee shall endorse its campus nominee to this award to the Education Council.

  1. LOYALTY AWARD. This award is given to students who have completed their studies at AMA Computer College since elementary. Students who have stopped during period of their studies are disqualified from receiving this award.
