GUIDELINES ON DISCIPLINARY ACTIONS

STUDENT DISCIPLINARY TRIBUNAL

 

     All complaints for violations of College rules and regulations are heard and investigated upon by the Student Disciplinary Tribunal. Based on its findings, it submits its recommendation to the Assistant Vice President, Chief Operating Officer regarding the action to be taken. The SDT is composed of the DEAN of the Student Affairs as presiding officer with one representative from the faculty and the studentry as members.
