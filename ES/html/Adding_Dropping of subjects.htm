ENROLMENT POLICIES

ADDING / DROPPING OF SUBJECTS

 

     A student who wishes to change / add/ drop subject/s should accomplish dropping / adding form which is available at the Registrar’s Office, provided however, that such change will not be in conflict with his other schedules. 

     Accomplish form should then be filed at the Accounting Office not later than the fourth (4th) day of classes or as scheduled by the Registrar to effect a refund. If form is filed after the fourth (4th) day, no refund will be effected.
