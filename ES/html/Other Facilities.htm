POLICY ON SCHOLASTIC DELINQUENCY

OTHER FACILITIES

 

AUDIO-VISUAL ROOMS

     Audio-Visual rooms were established to provide the faculty and the students with richer teaching – learning experience through the effective use of the instructional media. In the said rooms, we house, collect, organize and make available adequate instructional materials like graphics, slides, films, filmstrips, transparencies, overhead projectors, sound systems, tape recorders, Multi Media System.

NATURAL SCIENCE LABORATORIES

     The Main building houses a complete line of laboratory instruments and facilities for both chemical and physical sciences. The laboratories are under - all the supervision of the Natural Science Coordinator of the College of Arts and Sciences.

ROTC OFFICE

     The ROTC Unit offers a four trimester basic and advanced training course. Pursuant to the provision of National Defense Act as amended, all physically fit Filipino male student are required to render national service for the purpose of fulfilling educational requirements established by law.

   Objectives:

                   1. To train officers for reserve components and possible direct commission in the Armed Forces of the Philippines.

                   2. To train the students for the position of leadership in the defense establishment in the event of emergency.

                   3. To train officers and enlisted reservist potentials from the reserve components of the AFP (Armed Forces of the Philippines)

     There are four basic ROTC courses which will serve as one of the requirements for male students to finish the course such as MS 11, MS 12, MS 21 and MS 22.

BOOK AND SCHOOL SUPPLIES STORE

     Book and School supplies store are located near the canteen. Computer books and other reading materials are available at reasonable prices.

CAFETERIAS AND KIOSK

     The College has snack counter and kiosks located in the strategic places. There is a separate area for AMACC personnel and staff.

CHAPEL

     In order to uplift the spiritual life of students, faculty and employees in the campus, the Management built the AMACC Chapel. It is solemn place perfect for mediation, prayers and bible reading. Most of the activities that are being held in the Chapel are sponsored by the Student Catholic Action, a socio-religious student organization duly recognized by the Office of the Student Affairs. Masses are celebrated on the First Fridays at 9:00 am and during the special occasion of the College

CAMPUS GYMNASIUM AND SPORT FACILITIES

     Physical Education classes and other sports activities are conducted in the Gymnasium and P.E., rooms with facilities for the main sports. The college through the P.E. department conducts sport festival, recreational activities and selects athletes to represent the College in athletic competition. All bonafide students and employees of AMACC may use the various P.E. facilities provided there is a prior approval from the P.E. Coordinator

SWIMMING POOL

     The swimming pool is provided for P.E. classes. It located near the AMACC Chapel and at the back of the high school building. The swimming pool area can likewise be use for get together parties

SUPREME STUDENT COUNCIL AND OFFICIAL STUDENT PUBLICATION

     The student has a Student Council and its official publication, DATALINE. The student Council is composed of a President, Vice President and 7 to 9 Councilors who are elected popularly every school year. The Editorial Board member of DATALINE are selected annually by competitive examinations (news writing, lay-out and editorial writing). Both institution acquire its funds by fixed contribution of students. The contribution are given every trimester. The College plays an active role ensuring that the collection and spending of funds are properly made. Everyone is being encouraged to actively support the Council and Dataline.
