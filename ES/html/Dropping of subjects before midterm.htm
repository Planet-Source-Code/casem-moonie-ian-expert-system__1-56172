ENROLMENT POLICIES

DROPPING OF SUBJECTS BEFORE MIDTERM

 

     A grade of D (officially dropped) means the student has officially dropped the subject/s. Dropping of subject/s will not be allowed during and after the midterm examinations. Dropping of subjects should be approved by the Dean, Registrar and faculty member concerned. A grade of 5.00 shall be given to subject/s which is / are dropped unofficially.

     A student who incurred absences for more than 20% of the required total number of laboratory or lecture periods is given a grade of UD provided said absences were incurred before  the midterm examinations and that the students did not officially dropped the subject/s. If absences were after the midterm examinations, the final grade will then be 5.00 if the midterm grade is failing and UD is passing.
