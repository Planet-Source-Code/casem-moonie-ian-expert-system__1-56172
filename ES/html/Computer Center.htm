POLICY ON SCHOLASTIC DELINQUENCY

COMPUTER CENTER

 

     There is a separate 4 – story building for Computer Laboratory classes. There are 15 laboratories which can be found both in the first and second floors while the Digital and CAD laboratories and drafting room are found in the 3rd floors. Students are given the opportunity to use state-of-the-art computer facilities like the AS 400 and the CAD Laboratories.
