GENERAL POLICIES FOR STUDENTS

EXAMINATION GUIDELINES

 

     The following testing rules / procedures should be observed during examination:

  1. Wear ID properly.
  2. Bring your examination permit and clearly write the subject and section at the back of this document. A student shall not be allowed to take the examination without the permit.
  3. Be sure that the proctor signs the permit during the examination period.
  4. Place all the things except examination documents in front of the room.
  5. Come on time. Latecomers may be allowed to take the examination provided they will not extend the examination period.
  6. Honesty is always the best policy. Cheating shall be punished with a grace of 0 percentage in the examination.
  7. Leaving the room during examination shall not be allowed. Attend to your personal needs before the examination starts.
  8. Ensure that you sign the attendance sheet (2 copies) for each subject taken.

     All the conflicts in examination schedule should be settled within the examination week. Schedule of the examination for subjects with conflicts will be prepared and announced by the area Coordinator concerned. Students must notify their respective teachers of said conflict/s one week before the scheduled exam. No exam shall be administered to students who failed to notify their teachers about the conflict in schedule.

     Midterm exam in the laboratory is scheduled a week after the midterm examination week and the final exam is scheduled a week before the finals.
