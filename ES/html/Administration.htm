POLICY ON SCHOLASTIC DELINQUENCY

ADMINISTRATION
                    

For the purpose of implementing this rule, the following officers shall be having the following responsibilities.

1.1  Department Chairpersons – shall submit to the office of the Dean an advance copy of the lists of students with final grades below three (3). The office of the Dean shall then submit the complete list to the Registrar.

1.2  Registrar shall be primarily responsible for the monitoring the academic performance of students and shall mark upon the enrollment form of the applicable condition.

a. Notice                N            :must pass 50%

b. Probation           P            :must pass more than 75%

c. Dismissal            DS         :must pass all subjects

1.3  Guidance Counselor – shall implement the necessary program for assisting students who are given any of the aforementioned conditions and shall coordinate with the academic departments in so far as the tutorials of said students is concerned.
