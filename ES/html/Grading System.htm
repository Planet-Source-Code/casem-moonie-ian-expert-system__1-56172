            A student who failed to take the midterm examinations should be given 50% for the midterm examination.

Note:  NO “NFE” GRADE POLICY

            Student who failed to take the final examination shall be given a grade of NFE in a period of seven (7) working days to take a special examination. If however, the student still fails to take the examination, he shall be given a grade of 50% in a final examination for purpose of computing the final grade.
