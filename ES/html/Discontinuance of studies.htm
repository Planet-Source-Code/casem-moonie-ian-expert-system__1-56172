ENROLMENT POLICIES

DISCONTINUANCE OF STUDIES / LEAVE OF ABSENCE

 

     Discontinuance of studies results either through the issuance of transfer credentials Honorable Dismissal) issued by the Registrar’s Office for reasonable cause or upon petition of the student or an order of dismissal by the college President after the commission of a major offense specified in the Code of Conduct for students. A student who would like to leave the College temporarily must first secure permission from the

     Dean stating the definite period and the reason for his leave. He must accomplish the Leave of Absence Form which can be secured from the Registrar’s Office. Otherwise, the period of his absence shall be counted against him for purpose of maximum residency rule.
