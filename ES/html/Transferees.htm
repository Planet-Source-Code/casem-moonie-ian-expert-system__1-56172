ADMISSION POLICIES

TRANSFEREES
 

     A students who  has been enrolled in a post secondary course in another school and who desires to transfer to AMA shall be admitted if he has a previous general weighted averagge of 2.50 of its equivalent and if he has qualified in the AMA College Entrance test. All applicants shall be interviewed by the Guidance Counselor, the Dean of Students Affairs and Registrar for selection. The following documents are required when seeking admission:

1. Transcript of records or True Copies of Grades
2. Honorable Dismissal
3. Certificate of Good Moral Character
4. Letter of application of the  student
5. Letter of parent Consent
6.Two pictures (two 1” x 1”, two 2” x 2”, red  background.
