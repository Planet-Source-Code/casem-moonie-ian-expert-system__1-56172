GUIDELINES ON DISCIPLINARY ACTIONS

JURISDICTION AND VENUE 

     

     The Office of the Student Affaires of the College where the respondent student is officially enrolled as of the date of alleged commission of the offenses shall have jurisdiction over the case regardless of the place where said offenses allegedly or was proved to have been committed.

     Jurisdiction may however be transferred from one campus to another upon the approval of the President or his authorized representative. The Coordinator who made the request shall immediately inform the concerned parties of the approval of the transfer within three (3) schooldays.
