GUIDELINES ON DISCIPLINARY ACTIONS

OFFENSES

     

     Offenses are those pertaining to violation of any of the rules and regulations governing student conduct and discipline as contained in the Student Handbook. The set of guidelines to be distinguished from the rules and regulations governing scholastic delinquency. They are to be strictly considered as non-academic infractions and are therefore outside the jurisdiction of the officers of the academic departments. They are classified into major and minor offenses.
