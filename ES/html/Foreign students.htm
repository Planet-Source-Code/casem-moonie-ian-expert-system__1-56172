ADMISSION POLICIES

FOREIGN STUDENTS
    

Foreign students shall be admitted after passing the College Entrance Test and interview by the Registrar. Foreign students enrolling for the first time must submit the following requirements:
1. Photo copy of the Alien Certificate of Registration (resident alien)
2. Previous study permit (if transferee from Philippine School)
3. Accomplishment Personal History Statements (5 copies)
4. Certificate of bank deposits
5. Affidavit of support
6. Birth certificate
7. Transcript of Records (original and authenticated copies)
8. High School of Card (original and authenticated copies)
9. Seven (7) copies of 2” x 2” pictures
10. Two (2) copies of of 1” x 1” pictures (item 3 – 10 are  needed for Students Visa  Application)
