GENERAL POLICIES FOR STUDENTS

STUDENT ORGANIZATIONS

 

     Student organizations exist solely on the basis of camaraderie, unity and purposes of explicitly stated in the organization’s constitution. Duly recognized student organizations’ objective should be in the line with the College mission and objectives. Student who wish to form, establish and participate in student organization are allowed to do so but are subjected to reasonable rules and regulations as promulgated by the College authorities.

Office of the students Affairs (OSA) is responsible in implementing the following:

  1. Recognition and Suspending of Students Organizations as the need arises and in accordance with the violations as set forth by the Office of the Students Affairs. Application for recognition is schedule within the first two months of the first trimester of a particular school year.
  2.  Approval of faculty advisers as recommended by the officer of the students organizations.
  3. Approval and disapproval of student’s activity as reckoned from the College mission and institutional objectives.
  4. Mediates a renders a decision in cases of conflict between and among organizations.

REQUIREMENTS FOR RECOGNITION / RENEWAL OF STUDENTS ORGANIZATIONS.

  1. Applications Form to be secure from the Office of the Student Affairs within the required period (first two months of the first trimester).
  2. Constitution and By-Laws.
  3. Members – a composition of 10 – 20 for a start.
  4. Set of Interim Officer with pertinent information as Year Level, Courses, Address and Telephone Number.
  5. Annual Program of Activities.
  6. Progress report of organizations seeking renewal.
  7. Faculty Adviser /s
  8. Monetary Collection / Financial Report

BAN ON ALL FORMS OF FRATERNITIES AND / OR ILLEGAL ORGANIZATIONS

     All forms of fraternities and / or illegal organization are not allowed in this institution. Hence, all bonafide students of associations. Any student proven and / or illegal organization shall be subjected to by a severe sanction.
